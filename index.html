ABOUT US

The Vaidhya Group of Companies, headquartered at B28 Mahu Neemuch Road, District Mandsaur, Madhya Pradesh, India 458001, is a diversified conglomerate dedicated to fostering growth and making a positive impact across various sectors. Established in 2016, our journey began with a strong foundation in education, evolving into a multi-faceted organization.

Under the umbrella of the Vaidhya Group, Vaidhya Organisation Pvt. Ltd. is committed to empowering individuals through comprehensive education, training, and placement services. Simultaneously, Vaidshree Welfare Foundation drives our social initiatives, tirelessly working on projects that benefit communities and contribute to societal well-being. Our global reach is extended through Vaidhya Import Export, specializing in the trade of quality cooking oil and unique slate pencils. At our core, Vaidhya Group Mandsaur continues to serve as a leading educational institute, upholding our foundational commitment to knowledge and learning.

We are united by a common vision: to deliver excellence, foster innovation, and contribute meaningfully to the progress of individuals and society.

Our Mission

 Our mission at Vaidhya Group of Companies is to foster sustainable growth and positive societal impact across all our ventures. We aim to be a leader in each sector we operate in – from empowering through quality education and training, to uplifting communities through dedicated social welfare initiatives, and fostering global trade through reliable import-export services. We are committed to upholding the highest standards of integrity, innovation, and client satisfaction, ultimately striving to contribute significantly to the economic and social development of Mandsaur, Madhya Pradesh, and beyond.

At Vaidhya Group, we believe in building strong foundations – be it in education, business, or community welfare – to create a brighter, more prosperous future for all.

Our Vision

At Vaidhya Group of Companies, our vision is to foster holistic growth and sustainable development – empowering individuals through quality education and training, enriching communities through dedicated social initiatives, and connecting markets through reliable global trade. We strive to build a future where every endeavour contributes positively to society and the economy.
